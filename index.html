Our Featured Article

Assisting the COVID-19 Situation

In this Covid-19 season, Raydy Beehoon wants to show some love to people who need it the best way we can, through our beehoon sets! From 13th April to 4 May, Raydy is launching RAYDY GIVES, in collaboration with Food Bank SG. RAYDY GIVES will cook 300-500 packets of economical beehoon everyday for those in need. But we need your help. We’re currently running a crowd-funding campaign to cover the costs incurred for the procurement of ingredients & packaging, as well as the delivery costs (should there be a need). We would greatly appreciate any amount you can provide us with.


-Ray Sheng, RAYDY's Founder

We're Expanding...

Our team is growing and we are looking at expanding this business. Here's what you can expect!

Food Truck Deliveries

Tired of waiting in line for your food? We are launching our food vans that drives across NTU. No pre-orders, just head to the nearest pickup point and pickup your food.

Promotions

We are launching loyalty programs for our most loyal customers that eat us often. Do keep an eye out for this if you're our avid supper fan!

New Stalls

We are considering opening more supper stalls, including Indian (prata) and maybe Western cuisines?
